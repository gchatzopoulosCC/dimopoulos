The best places to eat in Thessaloniki

Thessaloniki is a vibrant, young, and multicultural huge city so it will obviously have many restaurant options. Here you can find everything you wish for and good food is very easy to find . Our city was names of one of the ‘Top 10 city breaks in Europe’ as per THE GUARDIAN many restaurants consist tradditional Greek music , while others offer Mediterranean cuisine with modern or even traditional dishes. Another positive about the restaurants here is that they always provide a warm welcome and friendly atmosphere. Below, you will find a list of the best restaurants in Thessaloniki offering a variety of dishes in every price range

01. Maiami Seafood Restaurant in Kalamaria, Thessaloniki

Miami Seafood Restaurant has definetly been one of the best restaurants in Thessaloniki for a very long time and it has never lost its glamor . It is located in the zone of Kalamaria beach . The restaurant consists two indoor lounges which are mostly used in the winter season , while you can also dine in the outdoor open patio in the summer. Here you will find international creative cuisine , new Greek cuisine but also classic recipes with fish and different seafood. The style of service will always make the customar feel very welcome and special , since the most important part of their philosophy is to satisfy the guests.

In order to book a table:

  • • Thetidos 18, Kalamaria Municipality, Thessaloniki, Greece

    • 02. To Manitari Restaurant in Kalamaria, Thessaloniki

    To Manitari Restaurant, is located in the East site of Thessaloniki , in Kalamaria. It offers a creative greek cuisine menu , best quality products and a menu which is presented by the awarded consultant chef Dimitris Pamporis. The menu differs from season to season in order to give their customers a great experience and an alternative to their preference. The beautiful balcony with the casual decoration, comfortable sofas, monastery style tables and chill out music, in combination with impeccable service of the restaurants team, makes you feel cozy and relaxed so as to enjoy your meal or even to drink a glass of good wine from Greece’s vineyards.

    In order to book a table:

  • To Manitari Restaurant in Kalamaria, Thessaloniki
  • Sofouli 57 – Thessaloniki – 55131 – Greece
  • +306907798620
    • 04. Mare i monti in Panorama,Thessaloniki

    If you like pasta then this is the place for you , even though it might be a little far from the city . Mare i monti is an Italian restaurant where you will enjoy traditional italian food. Here you will be offered a warm and cozy place to enjoy your meal. It is an ideal location where you can visit it either on your own or with your company . Also you will be highlighted and promoted the Italian gastronomic culture and comply with the quality of the Italian hospitality . You will certainly have a great time drinking a traditional italian glass of wine and enjoying an authentic tiramisu dessert after your meal.

    In order to book a table:

  • Mare e monti in Panorama, Thessaloniki
  • Venizelou, 13, Panorama – Ladadika – Thessaloniki – Greece
  • +302310343344
    • 05. Loupino Ladadika,Thessaloniki

    Loupino is one of the greatest restaurants in Thessaloniki as it offers a variety of dishes for vegetarians and meat lovers. So, whether you love eating meat or are even vegan, there are many dishes options. You will have the chance to try Greek dishes, and the chef loves to prepare them in a more modern way.

    The atmosphere is certainly warm and friendly as there also is an amazing aesthetic and luxury, and the staff is ready to help you choose the best dish for your tastes. Moreover, the restaurant is located next to the city port. So, after your meal, you can enjoy a walk in Thessaloniki’s Nea Paralia or New Waterfront area with the iconic White Tower. Best of all, it’s open late if you’re a late diner.

    In order to book a table:

  • Loupino in Ladadika, Thessaloniki
  • Katouni 4 – Ladadika – Thessaloniki – Greece
  • +302310535707
