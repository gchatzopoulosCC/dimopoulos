Information

Welcome to Gorilas Bar, a vibrant oasis nestled in the heart of Thessaloniki! We invite you to embark on a unique and unforgettable journey where the spirit of the city meets the untamed energy of the jungle. Step into our lively sanctuary, where the rhythmic beats of the urban jungle seamlessly blend with the warm, welcoming atmosphere of our bar. From expertly crafted cocktails to a diverse selection of beverages, Gorilas Bar promises an immersive experience that transcends the ordinary. Join us as we redefine the nightlife scene in Thessaloniki, creating memories as bold and powerful as the majestic gorillas that inspire our name. Cheers to adventure, camaraderie, and the extraordinary moments that unfold at Gorilas Bar!
