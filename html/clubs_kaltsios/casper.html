CASPER

Greek Music | 25-45 y.o. | 20€ p.p.

Casper, or Casper at Verykoko which is its full name, is one of the longest operating clubs in the city of Thessaloniki. It consists of a space for 900 people of all ages since it is a club with greek music. The club is open from Tuesday to Sunday after 23:00 and offers a plethora of events on various different days. On Tuesday you can enjoy popular Greek singer Ilias Kampakakis and on Sundays Giorgos Sampanis. The remaining days you can enjoy mainstream DJ's such as DJ Ferfiris. It is, in my opinion, the best club in Thessaloniki if you enjoy Greek Music.
