Apallou

About Apallou

Nestled next to the iconic Aristotle Square in the heart of Thessaloniki, Apallou is a vibrant and trendy bar that stands out as a must-visit destination in this historic city. With a chic and inviting ambiance, Apallou is a popular hangout known for its delectable brunch classics and an enticing array of international meals. Whether you're in the mood for a leisurely brunch or an evening filled with tantalizing cocktails and fine wine, Apallou has you covered. The bar offers a delightful outdoor seating area, allowing patrons to soak in the lively atmosphere of the square while indulging in the culinary delights and expertly crafted beverages. What sets Apallou apart is its commitment to entertainment, featuring live performances that add an extra layer of excitement to the overall experience. With a prime location and a diverse menu, Apallou captures the essence of Thessaloniki's culinary and social scene, making it a standout destination for locals and tourists alike.
